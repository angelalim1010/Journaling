Name: Zenyu

Platform: Browser that can be used on multiple screens because it's easier for people to write
Functionality: Users can write and choose a mood (like in apps like Flo), create, edit, delete, look at previous entries

Product Definition: 
I. Vision
    - To encourage/motivate individuals to lead a healthy lifestyle 
II. Target audience
    - Anyone who struggles with overwhelming emotion: e.g. stress, anxiety
    - All age groups
    - People who prefer privacy/don't want to verbally express themselves to someone else
III. User Problem
    - To better manage/cope with stress, anxiety, and mental health conditions
    - To provide an opportunity for positive self-talk
    - To identify negative thoughts and behaviors/stressors/triggers and learn ways to better control them
IV. Strategy
    - Browser-based web application that provides a structured approach to journaling
    - Allows users to express and privately reflect on their feelings
    - Offers safe boundaries and guidance
    - Enables users to come to a deeper understanding about themselves
V. Goals
    - We know if our product works when we see improvement of mood over time as people continually use the app
